About Us.

Our Story

One summer, in the midst of a pandemic, around a year ago at the time of writing this, a young boy named David had a dream, an idea, a breakthrough! He thought, since he had all this freetime because of the pandemic, why not try and learn coding. Should be fun, he said. And it was! This idea had been brought to David from his best friend. He enjoyed it so much that he made a cool website for a fake company. A month later, that fake company wasn't that fake anymore. It had turned into a multi hour, multi dollar business. David was finally happy after being a little bored for some months. Now, David Industries is the pride and joy of David, and has brought him much pleasure, and, best of all, money!

Our Staff
